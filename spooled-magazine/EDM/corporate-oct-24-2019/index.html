Current Spring Issue

Our Spring issue features a broad range of topics by some of the country’s most respected fishing writers.

Steve Starling has recently discovered the benefits of stand-up paddle boards in his estuary fishing pursuits. Stealth and extreme shallow water capabilities have endeared SUP’s to Starlo, and this great feature explains how, where and when to use them.

Glen Booth loves all things blue water, and his great mahi mahi expose reveals all you’ll need to know to catch them as offshore waters warm up.

New contributor, Jamie Crawford, provides a national round up of spring fishing opportunities, Jarrod Day puts Victorian snapper under the microscope, while Shane Mensforth takes an in-depth look at SA’s magnificent mulloway fishing. Boaties will be interested in John Willis’ new restoration project as he breathes new life into a well-used 19 year old Stabicraft 490.

We test the new Yellowfin 6500 and a whole lot more.
